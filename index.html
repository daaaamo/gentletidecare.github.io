Get in touch

Who we are

A small team. Real people. No cutting corners.

Gentle Tide Care is a small, multicultural support team on the Sunshine Coast. We're not a big agency — we're a tight group of experienced adults who take real pride in this work and genuinely care about the people we support.

We keep our team small on purpose. It means you get the same familiar faces, real relationships, and care that doesn't slip through the cracks.

How we help

What we do

Practical, everyday support — at home, out and about, and for families who need a break.

Help at home

  • Showering, bathing and dressing
  • Grooming and personal hygiene
  • Meal preparation and cooking
  • Light cleaning and laundry
  • Tidying and home organisation
  • Light gardening (paths, lawns)
  • Shopping — with you or on your behalf
  • Help with everyday tasks around the home

We provide practical, hands-on support. We do not administer medication or provide clinical nursing care.

Out in the community

  • Getting to appointments
  • Shops, markets, cafes and errands
  • Social groups and activities
  • Sports, recreation and hobbies
  • Visiting friends and family
  • Attending events and outings
  • Trying new activities and experiences
  • Building confidence and connection

We cover the support worker's time and travel. Entry fees and activity costs are the participant's own.

Respite & palliative support

  • In-home respite so carers can rest
  • Companionship and calm presence
  • Practical help around the home
  • Consistent support during serious illness
  • Daytime and overnight cover
  • Reliable short-notice availability where possible

We provide compassionate companionship and practical support — not clinical or nursing care.

Our promises

What you can count on

We show up. If we say we'll be there, we'll be there — no chasing, no last-minute let-downs.
Your choice, respected. We work hard to match you with people you connect with and to keep that consistency — and when something changes, we'll always tell you in advance, never just send a stranger.
We genuinely care. We're not here to tick boxes. We care about the people we support and you'll feel the difference.
Honest about what we do. We only promise what we can deliver. If something is outside our scope, we'll say so and help you find who can.
Quality, done properly. Proper documentation, proper agreements, proper care — nothing half-done.
You come first. Your wellbeing leads every decision — never rushed, never pressured, never treated like a task.

Our team

Chosen for skill, and for heart.

Our small team includes experienced support workers and a founder with years of hands-on care work. We're a multicultural group — most of us around 40 — with real life experience and a genuine commitment to doing this work well.

Between us we speak English, French, Spanish and Arabic — so if language matters for your participant or family, just ask.
